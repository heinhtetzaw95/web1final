I am going to graduate in a few days. I have been pursing my bachelor degree and it has come to the end. After all the years of studying and doing projects, I have gotten some experiences with programming languages, basic about computer architectures, operating systems, database management systems, computer networking, and designing principles as a result.

After my graduation, I still have to learn more technology to keep myself up to date with newer technologies. Entry level jobs are preferable for me to start out with since I am a new grad without much experiences. My motto is to make the software that I have to develop the best possible state without any flaws.

Since I am a perfectionist, I like to have fully functional, bug free experience for the users of my piece of software. I pay attention on most details that most people will not even bother putting effort into. That is also why I take a long time coding compared to most of my classmates. Moreover, I test what I develop in several different ways so that it is very reliable.

I will be very happy just if I can give a pleasant experience to the users of my software of web application. If my character matches what you are looking for as an employee, please download a copy of my resume on Resume page and also shoot me a short message.
