  • Life in the US

I was born in 1995 in the former capital city of my country. I lived there for about two years and my family moved to the upper part of the country. We lived there for about a year or two and moved back down to the lower part and lived for about two years.

I started to speak when we were living in upper part of the country and I picked up the accent from the locals. It is a kind of funny for the people in the lower part of the country, especially when a child speaks in the accent since it is very rarely seen. After that, we moved back down to the lower part again.

When I was about five, I remember going to pre-school in a town called Hinthada. My dad made some poems and rhymes for me and my little brother about being late for school. It was around 1999. We moved to another city called Daik-U in around 2000 and I started going to state school there. I made many friends there since we lived there for about eight years.

I finished primary school and middle school in Daik-U before we moved to another town called Phyu. The year 2007 was one of the milestones in my life because my dad bought a laptop and had a computer of for our family for the first time in our entire life. I was about 13 at that time.

If you want to know more, please send me an anonymous message by using my contact information that is provided on Hire Me page. ;)
