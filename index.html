Welcome to my portfolio website. On this site, you can visit and read about me, view my projects, judge my philosophy, scan my life timeline, and hire me by using my contact information provided.

This website is all about me. There are sections for you to read about my life experiences both in my country and in the United States. Experience to live in a foreign country away from family is one of the most exotic experiences. Living among people who I knew nothing about and became friends is very interesting. Moreover, most of the people I met are very kind and helpful.

You can read all about in the section where I put about my life in the United States as an international student. In the section about my life in Myanmar, which is my home country, you can read about how I lived with my family, how I went to school every day, my friends, and culture of the country. I am almost sure you will enjoy reading it even though it is written in my own view and you would have to see the country through my eyes and my interruption. Enjoy exploring about me, and thank you for visiting my website.
